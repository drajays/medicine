ⓘ About

How the algorithm works

This panel measures 13 steroids across the Δ4 and Δ5 cascades, evaluating substrate-to-product flux to identify enzymatic bottlenecks.

The engine checks for:

  • • Absolute cutoffs — LC-MS/MS thresholds
  • • Suppression patterns — deficient downstream products
  • • Diagnostic ratios — Cortisol/Cortisone, 17-OHP/Progesterone, DHEA/A4, (21DF+17OHP)/Cortisol
  • • Pathway shunting — precursor accumulation patterns
Conditions covered

CAH variants: 21-OHD (classic & NCCAH), 11β-OHD, 17α-OHD/CYP17A1, 3β-HSD2

Hyperandrogenism: PCOS (ovarian vs adrenal), virilizing tumor

Mineralocorticoid: Primary Aldosteronism, DOC-producing tumor, AME

Adrenal mass: ACC vs benign adenoma differentiation
