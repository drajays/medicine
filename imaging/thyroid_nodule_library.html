ATA-aligned quick thresholds – 2015/2025: High suspicion US >70–90% risk → FNA ≥10 mm. Intermediate 10–20% → ≥10 mm. Low 5–10% → ≥15 mm. Very low <3% → ≥20 mm / observe. Benign <1% → no FNA.
ACR TI-RADS: TR5 ≥10 mm, TR4 ≥15 mm, TR3 ≥25 mm FNA; follow TR5 ≥5 mm, TR4 ≥10 mm, TR3 ≥15 mm. Bethesda 2023: I-VI with ROM updated for NIFTP. Follow-up benign: high-suspicion US repeat US+FNA at 12 mo; low-intermediate 12–24 mo; very low >24 mo / may stop. Growth = ≥20% in 2 dimensions +2 mm, or ≥50% volume. – Free PDFs below.

Screening: high-risk history, TSH, neck US all compartments. FNA ATA: High/Intermediate ≥10 mm, Low ≥15 mm, Very low ≥20 mm, Benign no FNA. ACR: TR5 ≥10 mm, TR4 ≥15 mm, TR3 ≥25 mm. Follow benign: high-suspicion repeat US+FNA 12 mo; low-intermediate 12–24 mo; very low >24 mo / stop. Growth: ≥20% 2 dimensions +≥2 mm OR ≥50% volume. Surgical: Bethesda V/VI → surgery; Bethesda III/IV → repeat FNA / molecular / diagnostic lobectomy; compressive symptoms, ETE, N+, >4 cm → total thyroidectomy; 1–4 cm cN0 → lobectomy or total shared decision; <1–2 cm intrathyroidal → lobectomy / active surveillance. Non-surgical: benign symptomatic → RFA / laser / microwave / PEI cysts – ETA 2020/2023, Taiwan 2022, SFE 2022.
