📚 DSD / Pediatric Endocrinology Library

Important DSD papers & guidelines
with free article/PDF links

A quick-access HTML app for diagnosis and treatment of disorders/differences of sex development. Filter by topic, search titles/authors, open article pages, and download legal free PDFs when available.

Note: I included direct PDF buttons only for openly available/legal PDFs I could identify. For a few high-impact papers where no free PDF was found, the app links to the article/DOI page and labels it “article only”.
Suggested reading order for clinicians
  1. Initial approach: UK Society for Endocrinology 2021 guidance → Lancet diagnostic algorithm.
  2. Emergency/46,XX DSD: Endocrine Society CAH guideline → CAH review.
  3. Genetics: DSDnet molecular diagnosis paper.
  4. Long-term care: DSDnet standardized follow-up → psychosocial DSD-TRN paper → dsd-LIFE outcomes design.
  5. India context: Indian Association of Pediatric Surgeons DSD guideline.
Built as a static HTML file. External article/PDF links require internet access in a normal browser. Last updated: 1 July 2026.
